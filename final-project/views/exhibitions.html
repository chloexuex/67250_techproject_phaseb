Glitch & Flow: When Machines Dream

What happens when an algorithm is given a paintbrush? Glitch & Flow invites visitors into a world where artificial intelligence becomes a creative collaborator. Featuring generative works that shift and evolve in real time, this exhibition blurs the line between artist and machine — and asks who really holds the brush.

Signal: Designing the Invisible

From Wi-Fi waves to neural signals, Signal explores the invisible forces that shape our digital lives. Through large-scale projections, sound installations, and tactile displays, this exhibition transforms the abstract into the tangible — making the unseen impossible to ignore.
