Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is where art meets the future. We are a contemporary museum built around one idea: that technology is the most powerful creative tool of our time. From AI-generated installations to interactive digital sculptures, every exhibition at MonoMuse is designed to challenge how you see the world. Come curious. Leave inspired.

Want to know more about what we offer?

MonoMuse features three permanent galleries, two rotating exhibition spaces, a digital lab open to the public, and a rooftop event space. We host weekly artist talks, workshops for all ages, and a monthly late-night event series. Whether you're here for an hour or a full day, there's always something new to discover.
